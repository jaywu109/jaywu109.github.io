Bio
I am an incoming Ph.D. student in Computer Science at University of Utah in Spring 2026 under the supervision of Prof. Kenneth Marino. Currently, I am interning at SHENNONGSHI.AI, focusing on robot learning for cooking applications, and also collaborating with NTU Robot Learning Lab, advised by Prof. Shao-Hua Sun. My research focuses on robotics and autonomous agents.

Previously, I worked at KAUST in Saudi Arabia, with Prof. Bernard Ghanem and Dr. Guohao Li, and at Academia Sinica with Dr. Jun-Cheng Chen.
